Profile

I am a motivated and hardworking individual who will always look to find the most efficient and successful solution to any given problem, big or small. I work well in a team and can implement different ideas to help achieve the full potential of a project. I also work very well independently and always undertake tasks to the best of my ability. Employment & Experience

I worked as a cashier for G4S handling large volumes of secure cash and sensitive data and documents. This has contributed to my ability to maintain professionalism and security when working with others' data.

Being part of a team as a Crew Member at McDonald's improved upon my ability to work with others and manage my time effectively.

Volunteering at Sutton Community taught me the value in giving back to the community and how rewarding work can be.

Taking part in the National Citizen Service (NCS) massively improved my social skills and helped me network with people that I am still friends with to this day.
